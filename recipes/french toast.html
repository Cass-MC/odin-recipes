French Toast


The greatest breakfast dish of all time. Nothing else needs saying.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk the eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon.
  2. Dip slices of bread into the mixture.
  3. Cook on pan until golden brown on both sides.
