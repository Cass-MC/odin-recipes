Pumpkin Cheesecake


Delicious dessert for whenever fall decides to arrive.

Ingredients (Crust)

  • 1.5 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 6 tbsp melted, unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Ingredients (Filling)

  • 3 8oz packages of cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1.5 cups packed light brown sugar
  • 15oz canned pumpkin pie mix
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp real vanilla extract

Instructions (Crust)

  1. Beat graham crackers into fine crumbs.
  2. Stir 1.5 cups graham cracker crumbs, 1 tbsp sugar, 1/2 tsp cinnamon, and 6 tbsp melted butter. Transfer to a 9-inch springform pan with 3" walls and use a large spoon to press crumbs down, going about 1/2" up the sides. Bake at 350 degrees for 8 minutes, and remove to cool.

Instructions (Filling)

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. With a mixer, beat 3 packages of softened cream cheese and 1.5 cups brown sugar until light and fluffy, without lumps.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together 15oz pumpkin pie mix, 4 large eggs, 1/4 cup sour cream, 2 tbsp flour, 2 tsp pumpkin pie spice, 1/4 tsp salt and 1 tbsp vanilla extract. Mix well. Add to the other bowl and mix until well combined.
  3. Transfer filling to the crust and bake on middle rack for 1 hour. Turn off heat, prop open door slightly, and let sit for 45min. Remove and cool to room temp before covering and refrigerating. Serve when fully chilled- remove from pan by running a blunt knife along the sides to release from the pan.
