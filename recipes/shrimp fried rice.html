Shrimp Fried Rice


Fried rice with shrimp, bean sprouts, and onion. The chinese place I used to live next to has been dearly missed.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups uncooked white rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1.5 cups cooked medium shrimp (deveined, tail removed)
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes. Set aside to cool.
  2. Heat large skillet for 2 minutes. When hot, pour in vegetable oil, bean sprouts, onions. Mix well and cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Mix in cooled rice and cook for 3 minutes. Stir constantly.
  4. Mix in green onions, eggs, salt, pepper, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Cook for 4 minutes, stirring until eggs are cooked and everything is blended.
